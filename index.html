Noteify


A web application for SFU students to view and share course notes online.

Students can login and search for course notes that their peers have uploaded. They can rate, comment and download notes to help them study.

Random Encounters


Tired of going on quests and adventures with strangers you'll never meet halfway across the world?

Random Encounters is a web application that helps gamers find friends near their area to buddy up and game with. The site has an integrated Google Maps API that allows users to search up gamers who play similar games near them.
